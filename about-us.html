About Us

Our Heritage

The Shri Raghunath Bhatt Goswami Peeth (Bhatt Ji Ki Haveli) in Vrindavan is a cornerstone of Gaudiya Vaishnavism. For centuries, it has served as a sanctuary for sacred wisdom and ancient manuscripts that detail the philosophical and devotional traditions of the lineage.


Our mission at Shriraghunathfoundation.org is to preserve this priceless heritage. In collaboration with the Bhaktivedanta Research Center, Kolkata, we have undertaken the monumental task of digitizing these manuscripts, ensuring that the legacy of Shri Raghunath Bhatt Goswami is available to scholars, devotees, and seekers across the globe for generations to come.
