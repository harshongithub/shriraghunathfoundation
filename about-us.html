About Us

The Shri Raghunath Bhatt Goswami Peeth (Bhatt Ji Ki Haveli) in Vrindavan is a cornerstone of spiritual and literary heritage. Our foundation is dedicated to the preservation, digitization, and global accessibility of sacred manuscripts that have been safeguarded for centuries.
